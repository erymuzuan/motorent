# Project Milestones: MotoRent

## v1.0 Cashier Till & End of Day Reconciliation (Shipped: 2026-01-21)

**Delivered:** Full multi-currency cash management solution for Thailand's tourist rental market with till operations, manager oversight, and end-of-day reconciliation.

**Phases completed:** 1-9 (29 plans total)

**Key accomplishments:**

- Multi-currency cash handling (THB, USD, EUR, CNY) with exchange rate conversion and per-currency balance tracking
- Denomination counting for accurate float verification at open and close
- Transaction search workflow with fullscreen item confirmation and editing
- Split payment terminal mixing cash across currencies and payment methods
- Refunds and voids with manager PIN approval and compensating entry audit trail
- Manager oversight dashboard with active/closed sessions, variance alerts, and shift handover reports
- End of day operations with daily close locking, cash drop verification, shortage logging, and summary reports
