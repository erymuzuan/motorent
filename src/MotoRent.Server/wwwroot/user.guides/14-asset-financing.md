# Asset Financing: Tracking Your Fleet's Hire Purchase ROI

Are you expanding your fleet through **Hire Purchase (HP)**? In Malaysia, most rental businesses use bank financing to grow. But do you know exactly how much each vehicle is costing you every month after interest? 

JaleOs helps you track your loan principal, interest, and monthly installments, giving you a clear picture of when your vehicle will start generating pure profit.

![Hero: Professional Car Rental Office with HP Document and RM Coins](images/14-asset-financing-hero.png)

## How It Works in 30 Seconds

The **Asset Loan** module integrates your financing costs into your overall business analytics.

1.  **Enter Loan Details**: Input the principal amount, interest rate (APR), and term (months).
2.  **Auto-Schedule**: JaleOs generates a full amortization schedule for the entire loan period.
3.  **Track Payments**: Record monthly installments as part of your fleet expenses.
4.  **ROI Analysis**: Compare rental income vs. financing costs to see your true net profit.

## Story: Nizam's Fleet Expansion in Sabah

Nizam owns a rental company in Kota Kinabalu. He recently added five Proton X50s to his fleet, all financed through a 7-year bank loan.

*   **The Problem**: Nizam was seeing a lot of cash coming in, but he wasn't sure if he was actually making money after paying the RM1,100 monthly installment and insurance for each car.
*   **The Solution**: Nizam used the **Asset Financing** module to track each car's loan.
*   **The Result**: JaleOs showed him that during the low season, two of his cars were barely covering their interest. He decided to move those cars to a different branch in Sandakan where demand was higher, turning a potential loss into a profit.

## The Numbers: Why Tracking Interest Matters

Interest is a hidden cost that eats your profit. Let's look at a 5-year loan for a RM55,000 Perodua Ativa:

| Year | Monthly Rental Income | HP Installment | Interest Portion | **True Profit (Net)** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Year 1** | RM2,500 | RM850 | RM150 | **RM1,500** |
| **Year 5** | RM1,800 | RM850 | RM20 | **RM930** |

Even though the installment is the same, JaleOs helps you see that you're building more "equity" in the car as the years go by.

## Quick Setup

1.  **Select Vehicle**: Navigate to **Fleet > Vehicle List** and select the vehicle.
2.  **Add Loan**: Click on the **Financing** tab and select **Add New Loan**.
3.  **Enter Terms**: 
    *   **Principal**: e.g., RM50,000
    *   **Interest Rate**: e.g., 3.5% (Flat or Reducing)
    *   **Term**: e.g., 84 Months (7 Years)
4.  **Generate Schedule**: Click **Generate Amortization** to see your monthly breakdown.

## Day-to-Day Operations

*   **Payment Reminders**: JaleOs alerts you when a loan installment is due.
*   **Expense Integration**: Monthly installments are automatically added to the vehicle's "Total Cost of Ownership" (TCO).
*   **Early Settlement**: Calculate your "Rebate" if you decide to pay off the loan early to sell the vehicle.

## Optimize Your Fleet

*   **Expansion Planning**: Use the "Profit After Financing" report to see which car models pay themselves off the fastest.
*   **Leasing vs. HP**: Compare the costs of Hire Purchase against long-term leasing options.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Installment doesn't match bank** | Ensure you are using the correct interest type (Flat Rate is common for Malaysian HP). |
| **Loan not showing in reports** | Check if the loan status is set to "Active". |
