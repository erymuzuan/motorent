# SaaS Onboarding & Setup Guide

Welcome to the MotoRent SaaS platform! This guide will walk you through the process of setting up your organization and your first rental shop.

## 1. Creating Your Account

1. Go to the MotoRent landing page and click **Get Started** or **Start Free Trial**.
2. Sign up using your **Google** or **Microsoft** account.
3. Once logged in, you will be automatically redirected to the **Onboarding Wizard**.

## 2. The Onboarding Wizard

The wizard consists of 4 simple steps to get your business live:

### Step 1: Organization Profile
- **Organization Name**: Your business name (e.g., "Patong Rentals Co.").
- **Country & Language**: Set your primary operating region.
- **Currency**: Choose your primary currency (usually THB for Thailand).

### Step 2: Your First Shop
- **Shop Name**: e.g., "Patong Beach Branch".
- **Address & Contact**: This will appear on your receipts and agreements.
- **Operating Hours**: Set when your shop is open for pickups and returns.

### Step 3: Initial Fleet Setup
- You can quickly add your first few vehicles here.
- Select the **Vehicle Type** (Motorbike, Car, etc.).
- Enter the **Make/Model**, **License Plate**, and **Daily Rate**.
- You can add more vehicles later from the main Fleet menu.

### Step 4: Choose a Plan
- **Free**: For very small fleets (up to 5 vehicles).
- **Pro**: Includes advanced features like Agent Management, Document Templates, and Dynamic Pricing.
- **Ultra**: For large multi-shop operations.
- *Tip: All new accounts start with a 30-day Pro trial!*

## 3. Post-Onboarding Checklist

Once you complete the wizard, we recommend these next steps:

1. [ ] **Upload Your Logo**: Go to **Settings > Shop Settings** and upload your brand logo for receipts.
2. [ ] **Configure Insurance**: Set up your standard insurance packages in **Settings > Insurance**.
3. [ ] **Design Your Agreement**: Visit **Settings > Document Templates** to customize your rental contract.
4. [ ] **Open Your Till**: Go to the **Till** page to set your opening float and start processing rentals!

## Need Help?

Our support team is available via the chat widget in the bottom right corner of the application, or you can browse the full documentation at `/learn`.
