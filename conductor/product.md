# Initial Concept

A comprehensive motorbike rental management system (MotoRent) including rental tracking, vehicle maintenance, and renter management.

---

# Product Definition: MotoRent

## Vision
MotoRent aims to be the leading platform for motorbike rentals, providing a robust management system for shop owners and a seamless, modern experience for renters. By combining utility-focused administrative tools with an engaging customer interface, MotoRent bridges the gap between complex fleet logistics and the freedom of the road.

## Target Users
- **Rental Shop Managers and Staff:** The primary operators who use the system to manage inventory, process bookings, and handle the day-to-day logistics of a rental business.
- **Motorbike Renters (Tourists/Locals):** Customers looking for a reliable and easy way to find, book, and manage their vehicle rentals.

## Core Goals
- **Efficient Rental Management:** Streamline operations by providing tools for inventory tracking, reservation handling, and business oversight.
- **Seamless Renter Experience:** Deliver a user-friendly interface that makes finding and booking a motorbike effortless for customers.
- **Comprehensive Maintenance Tracking:** Ensure vehicle safety and reliability through automated tracking of service schedules and repair history.

## MVP Key Features
- **Real-time Inventory & Booking:** A live system to track vehicle availability and manage reservations dynamically.
- **Automated Maintenance Alerts:** Proactive notifications for scheduled maintenance based on time or vehicle usage, ensuring the fleet remains in top condition.
- **Visual Document Template Designer:** A drag-and-drop tool for creating and customizing professional document templates (Booking Confirmations, Rental Agreements, Receipts) with dynamic data binding.

## Branding & Visual Identity
- **Professional & Trustworthy:** The interface conveys reliability and stability, essential for managing business operations and building customer trust.
- **Modern & Vibrant:** A visually engaging aesthetic that captures the excitement of travel and the modernity of the platform.
