# Dynamic Pricing: Maximizing Revenue During Festive Seasons

Are you still charging the same price for a Perodua Myvi during Hari Raya as you do on a quiet Tuesday in October? If so, you're leaving thousands of Ringgit on the table.

In the Malaysian rental market, demand isn't constant. Between school holidays, Chinese New Year, and the year-end Langkawi peak, your vehicles are worth significantly more at certain times of the year. **Dynamic Pricing** automates your rate adjustments so you never miss out on peak-season profits.

![Hero: Car Rental Shop in Langkawi with Festive Calendar](images/13-dynamic-pricing-hero.webp)

## How It Works in 30 Seconds

Dynamic Pricing automatically adjusts your base rental rates based on rules you define.

1.  **Set the Rule**: Choose a date range (e.g., Hari Raya week) or recurring days (e.g., every Saturday).
2.  **Define the Adjustment**: Apply a multiplier (e.g., 1.5x for +50%) or a fixed amount (e.g., +RM50).
3.  **Automate**: JaleOs applies these rates across your booking portal and staff check-in wizard instantly.

## Quick Setup

To start maximizing your revenue, follow these steps:

1.  **Enable Feature**: Go to **Settings > Organization Settings** and ensure "Dynamic Pricing" is enabled.
2.  **Add a Rule**: Navigate to **Settings > Pricing Rules** and click **Add Rule**.
3.  **Configure**:
    *   **Name**: e.g., "Hari Raya 2026 Peak"
    *   **Type**: Select "Event" or "Season".
    *   **Dates**: Set your start and end dates.
    *   **Multiplier**: Enter `1.30` for a 30% increase.
4.  **Priority**: If you have overlapping rules, the one with the higher **Priority** number wins.

## Day-to-Day Operations

*   **Calendar View**: Use the **Pricing Calendar** to see exactly what the effective rate is for any day in the future.
*   **Manual Overrides**: You can still manually change a price during the check-in process if you need to give a special discount.
*   **Vehicle Specifics**: You can set rules that only apply to "Luxury" cars or "Vans" while keeping your "Economy" cars at standard rates.

## Optimize Your Pricing

*   **Weekend Boosters**: Set a recurring "Saturday/Sunday" rule for a +10% boost for weekend travelers.
*   **Long-Term Discounts**: While Dynamic Pricing handles peaks, use **Duration Discounts** to encourage 7+ day rentals during the off-season.
*   **Early Bird Rules**: Create rules that apply only if the booking is made far in advance.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Price not changing** | Check if the rule is set to "Active" and the dates overlap correctly. |
| **Wrong rule applied** | Check the **Priority** value. Higher numbers take precedence. |
| **Too expensive?** | Use the **Max Rate** setting to cap how high the price can go, no matter the multiplier. |
