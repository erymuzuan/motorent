# MotoRent Quick Start Guide - Mechanic

Welcome to MotoRent! This guide will help you get started as a Mechanic.

![Fleet Management](https://motorent.public.s3.amazonaws.com/user-guides/images/05-fleet.png)

## Your Role

As a Mechanic, you focus on fleet maintenance:
- View vehicle inventory
- Track vehicle status
- Manage accessories
- Monitor maintenance needs

## Navigation Overview

Your navigation menu includes:

| Menu | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Dashboard** | Overview of fleet status |
| **Fleet** | View all vehicles and accessories |

> **Note:** Rental operations, finance, and settings are managed by Staff and Managers.

## Getting Started

### 1. Dashboard Overview

Your Dashboard shows:
- **Active Rentals** - Vehicles currently out (view only)
- **Available Vehicles** - Ready for rental
- **Due Today** - Expected returns
- **Today's Revenue** - Revenue info (view only)

### 2. Fleet Management

Navigate to **Fleet > All Vehicles** to view the inventory.

#### Viewing Vehicles

The fleet page displays:
- **Vehicle Information** - License plate, brand, model
- **Type** - Motorbike, Car, Jet Ski, Boat, Van
- **Pricing** - Daily rate and deposit
- **Status** - Current availability
- **Location** - Which shop the vehicle is at

#### Vehicle Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Available** | Ready to rent |
| **Rented** | Currently with a customer |
| **Maintenance** | Being serviced - not available |

#### Filtering Vehicles

Use filters to find specific vehicles:
- **All** - Show all vehicles
- **Motorbike** - Two-wheelers only
- **Car** - Cars and sedans
- **Jet Ski** - Water vehicles
- **Boat** - Boats
- **Van** - Vans and large vehicles

Additional filters:
- **Status** - Filter by availability
- **Brand** - Filter by manufacturer
- **Include Pooled** - Show vehicles shared across shops

### 3. Accessories

Navigate to **Fleet > Accessories** to view rental accessories:
- Helmets
- Phone holders
- GPS devices
- Child seats
- Other equipment

Check accessory inventory and condition.

## Daily Tasks

### Morning Inspection
- [ ] Review vehicles returned yesterday
- [ ] Check for reported damage
- [ ] Verify vehicle availability

### During the Day
- [ ] Monitor maintenance status
- [ ] Update vehicle conditions
- [ ] Check accessory inventory

### Maintenance Tracking

Look for vehicles needing attention:
- Vehicles marked for maintenance
- Accident-damaged vehicles
- Regular service intervals

Report to your manager if:
- A vehicle needs urgent repair
- Parts are required
- A vehicle should be taken offline

## Understanding Vehicle Types

### Motorbikes
- Most common rental type
- Check: Tires, brakes, lights, mirrors
- Typical issues: Scratches, mirror damage

### Cars
- Higher deposit requirements
- Check: Body condition, interior, AC
- Typical issues: Dents, interior stains

### Jet Skis
- Interval-based rentals (hourly)
- Check: Hull, engine, safety equipment
- Typical issues: Hull scratches, engine maintenance

### Boats
- May require captain/guide
- Check: Hull, engine, safety gear, fuel
- Typical issues: Marine wear, engine service

### Vans
- Largest vehicles
- Check: All car items plus cargo area
- Typical issues: Higher wear and tear

## Reporting Issues

If you find a vehicle problem:

1. Note the license plate and issue
2. Report to your Shop Manager
3. They will:
   - Update the vehicle status
   - Create a maintenance record
   - Schedule repairs

## Tips for Success

1. **Be thorough** - Check vehicles carefully after returns
2. **Document everything** - Take photos of damage
3. **Stay organized** - Use filters to track your work
4. **Communicate** - Report issues promptly to management

## Need Help?

Contact your Shop Manager for:
- Vehicle status updates
- Maintenance scheduling
- Parts ordering
- Priority decisions
