# Safe & Go Offline Access Guide

This guide explains how to save your rental information for offline access, allowing you to view important details even without internet connection.

## Why Use Offline Access?

When traveling in Thailand, you may encounter:
- Areas with limited mobile data coverage
- Situations where you need quick access to shop contact information
- Times when you want to conserve mobile data

The offline feature lets you download your rental details to your phone, accessible anytime without internet.

## Downloading Your Rental

### Step 1: Access Your Rental Page

1. Open the link provided by the rental shop (QR code or URL)
2. Navigate to your active rental page
3. You'll see the "Save for Offline" card on the right side

### Step 2: Download the Data

1. Look for the **"Save for Offline"** card
2. Click the **"Download Now"** button
3. Wait for the download to complete (usually 1-2 seconds)

### Step 3: Confirm Download

After downloading, you'll see:
- **"Saved Offline"** header with a cloud icon
- **"Available offline"** status with a green checkmark
- **"Last synced"** showing when data was last updated
- **"Sync Latest"** button for refreshing the data

## What's Saved Offline?

The offline package includes:

### Your Rental Details
- Vehicle information (brand, model, license plate)
- Rental dates (pickup and return)
- Pricing details (daily rate, total amount)
- Start mileage
- Rental reference number

### Shop Contact Information
- Shop name and address
- Phone number (tap to call)
- Email address
- GPS location for navigation
- Operating hours

### Emergency Information
- Emergency contact numbers
- Instructions for reporting issues

## Using Offline Mode

### Viewing Saved Data

When offline, your rental page will:
1. Load from saved data automatically
2. Show all rental details and shop contacts
3. Display a notice that you're viewing cached data

### Contact Buttons

Even offline, you can:
- **Call** - Tap the phone number (requires phone signal, not data)
- **Navigate** - Opens saved GPS coordinates in your maps app
- **LINE** - Opens LINE app with saved shop ID

### Syncing Updates

When you regain internet connection:
1. Visit your rental page
2. Click **"Sync Latest"** to refresh data
3. The "Last synced" timestamp will update

## Troubleshooting

### "Download Now" Not Working

If the download fails:
1. Ensure you have internet connection
2. Refresh the page and try again
3. Clear browser cache if problem persists

### Data Shows "Stale"

If data is outdated:
1. Connect to internet
2. Click **"Sync Latest"** button
3. Data will refresh from server

### Offline Features Not Available

Some browsers may not support offline storage:
- Use Chrome, Safari, or Firefox for best experience
- Enable cookies and local storage in browser settings

## Best Practices

### Before Your Trip

1. Save your rental page as a bookmark
2. Download for offline access while on WiFi
3. Test offline mode before leaving your hotel

### During Your Rental

1. Sync data daily if you have internet
2. Keep the browser tab available (don't clear data)
3. Add the page to your home screen for quick access

### Adding to Home Screen

**iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open your rental page
2. Tap Share button
3. Select "Add to Home Screen"

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Open your rental page
2. Tap Menu (three dots)
3. Select "Add to Home screen"

## Privacy & Data

- Your rental data is stored locally on your device
- No personal information is sent to third parties
- Data is automatically cleared when rental completes
- You can manually clear data in browser settings

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much storage does it use?

Very minimal - typically less than 1 MB. The data includes text and QR codes only.

### How long is data kept?

Data remains stored until:
- You clear your browser data
- The rental is completed
- You manually delete it

### Does it work without any signal?

Yes! Once downloaded, you can view your rental details completely offline. However, to make calls or navigate, you'll need phone signal or GPS.

### Can I download multiple rentals?

Yes, if you have multiple active rentals, each can be saved for offline access.
